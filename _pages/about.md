Hello! My name's Dominic Behrens. I'm an economist based in Sydney, Australia,
currently working as a Senior Economist at The Centre for International
Economics. My work focuses on housing, land-use, transport and urban economics.
I combine quantitative analysis, modelling and large datasets with policy
communication for non-technical audiences.

In 2026, I worked with Grattan Institute as an Associate on *Wasted Space: Axe
Car-Parking Rules to Ease the Housing Crisis*, developing machine-learning
evidence on on-street parking supply and utilisation in Australian capital
cities. Before joining CIE, I worked in transport and urban economics at
Deloitte Access Economics.

I completed a Bachelor of Economics (Honours) at the University of New South
Wales, with my thesis applying econometric methods to estimate the impact of
high-speed internet on election results in Australia.

Outside work, I co-founded Sydney YIMBY and continue to serve as Secretary,
advocating for more affordable, sustainable and liveable cities.
